Porject Overview:
1. Visualize the relationship between global happiness and different factors across fields, such as public policy, public health, economics and etc..
2. Visualize the trend of covid-19 mortality rates.
3. Visualize the trend of vaccination rates.
4. Combine the world happiness with covid-19 deaths and vaccination rates, and see how the latter two influence the happiness index.
